### Not Professional Coaching

Output from `paced` is a **reference schedule** generated by combining your
historical training data with a periodization template you selected. It is
not a substitute for a qualified running coach, triathlon coach, strength
and conditioning specialist, or any other certified professional.

The software has **no knowledge** of:

- Your current physical condition, fatigue, sleep quality, or stress level
- Pre-existing injuries, chronic conditions, or medications
- Environmental factors (heat, altitude, air quality)
- Your biomechanics, movement patterns, or injury history
- Life circumstances that affect training availability

### Cardiac Monitoring Users

If you have enabled `cardiac_monitoring: true` in your athlete profile,
you have indicated that cardiac health is a concern. This flag causes the
system to apply additional conservatism to intensity recommendations.
However, this **does not constitute cardiac monitoring** in any medical
sense. If you have a known or suspected cardiac condition, work with a
cardiologist and do not rely on this software for health decisions.
